Our Legacy

Celebrating a remarkable 40-year journey since 1984, Ecocraft stands as a leading force in the interior infrastructure industry. With an installed capacity of 4.50 million sheets per annum, we deliver products that represent invaluable investments for our customers.

Our commitment extends beyond quality to include lifetime assurance and sustainable practices that have defined our legacy for four decades.
