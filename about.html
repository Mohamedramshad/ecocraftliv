Our Story

Building Dreams Since 1984

What began as a small plywood manufacturing unit in 1984 has today evolved into one of India's most trusted names in interior infrastructure. With four decades of unwavering commitment to quality, we've grown alongside the nation's development.

Our journey is a testament to Indian craftsmanship, innovation, and sustainable practices. From humble beginnings to becoming an industry leader, every step has been driven by our core values of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
